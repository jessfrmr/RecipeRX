Fatigue Relief

Curated recipes with the goal to fight fatigue

Revitalize & Energize

Dealing with persistent fatigue can be draining, but our selection of recipes is here to recharge your energy levels. Whether you're facing the weariness of a hectic lifestyle, chronic fatigue, or simply seeking a natural boost, these carefully curated recipes aim to replenish your vitality.

Avocado + Chickpea Quinoa Salad

This nutrient-packed quinoa salad is a powerhouse of energy-boosting ingredients, combining protein-rich quinoa, creamy avocado, and fiber-filled chickpeas to provide sustained energy throughout the day.

Avocado + Chickpea Quinoa Salad
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 can of chickpeas, drained
  • 1 cup spring mix
  • 1 can of black beans, drained
  • 1/2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
Directions:
  1. Layer the spring mix and cooked quinoa in a small bowl
  2. Add in the chickpeas, black beans, cherry tomatoes and mix
  3. Top with avocado and your favorite dressing

Green Smoothie

Packed with leafy greens and fruits, this energizing green smoothie offers a burst of vitamins and minerals to revitalize your energy levels and combat fatigue.

Green Smoothie
Ingredients:
  • 1 1/2 cups unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup fruit of choice
  • Protien powder (optional)
Directions:
  1. Place all ingredients in a blender
  2. Start blending on low speed then increase to high speed for 50-60 seconds or until smooth
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

Lemon + Dill Baked Salmon

This protein-rich baked salmon dish, infused with zesty lemon and aromatic dill, is a flavorful and energizing meal that provides essential omega-3 fatty acids to combat fatigue and support overall well-being.

Lemon + Dill Baked Salmon
Ingredients:
  • 1 8oz salmon filet
  • 1 tbsp avocado oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 small lemon
  • Sea salt + black pepper to taste
Directions:
  1. Drizzle the filet with the oil and season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle dill and top with lemon slices
  2. Transfer the salmon to a baking sheet and place in a 400 degree preheated oven
  3. Bake for about 12-15 minutes. Serve with your favorite sides and enjoy!

SPINACH
