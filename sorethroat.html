Sore Throat Relief

Curated recipes with the goal to soothe your sore throat

Comforting Remedies

A sore throat can be a pesky discomfort, but our collection of comforting recipes aims to relieve any irritation. From soothing beverages to nourishing soups and teas, each recipe is crafted to ease the discomfort of a sore throat, offering warmth, hydration, and a touch of soothing goodness to help you feel better in no time.

Chamomile + Lavender Tea

This herbal tea blend of chamomile and lavender, sweetened with raw honey, provides gentle relief and relaxation for a sore throat.

Chamomile + Lavender Tea
Ingredients:
  • 1 tsp dried chamomile
  • 1/2 tsp dried lavender
  • 1 1/2 tsp fresh mint
  • 1 cup boiling water
Directions:
  1. Bring 1 cup of water to a boil
  2. Combine boiling water, chamomile, lavender, and fresh mint. Let steep for 15 minutes
  3. Strain into your favorite mug and enjoy!

Carrot + Ginger Soup

This creamy and nourishing soup, infused with the warming flavors of carrot and ginger, provides both comfort and relief for a sore throat.

Carrot + Ginger Soup
Ingredients:
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1 lb carrots, roughly chopped
  • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 medium onion, chopped
Directions:
  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add in the onion, garlic, carrots, and salt and pepper. Cook until softened
  2. Add in the ginger and broth. Bring to a boil then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes
  3. Transfer to a blender and blend until smooth
  4. Pour into a bowl and garnish with coconut milk if deseired

Butternut Squash + Lentil Soup

This hearty soup, rich in vitamin C, beta-carotene, and antioxidants, provides warmth and nourishment and is perfect for comforting a sore throat.

Butternut Squah + Lentil Soup
Ingredients:
  • 3 carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, smashed
  • 1 medium butternut squash, diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Dried thyme and oregano to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups brown or green lentils
Directions:
  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add in the onion, garlic, carrots, and salt and pepper. Cook until softened
  2. Add in the butternut squash, lentils, oregano, thyme and broth. Bring to a boil then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes
  3. When the squash and lentils are tender, pour into a bowl and enjoy!
