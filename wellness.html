Rainbow Veggie Stir-Fry

This colorful and nutrient-packed stir-fry features an array of vibrant vegetables, providing a burst of flavors and essential nutrients for overall well-being.

Rainbow Veggie Stir-Fry
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup peanut sauce
  • 1/2 head red cabbage
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar snap peas
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 8 oz rice noodles, cooked
  • 1 cup bell peppers, chopped
Directions:
  1. In a large fry pan melt oil over medium heat. Add in the red cabbage, sugar snap peas, carrots, and bell peppers, saute for a couple minutes
  2. Once the veggies are done, add in the cooked rice noodles
  3. Plate, top with peanut sauce and enjoy!

Baked Lemon Herb Chicken

This tender and flavorful baked chicken, infused with zesty lemon and aromatic herbs, offers a satisfying and protein-rich meal for overall wellness.

Baked Lemon Herb Chicken
Ingredients:
  • 4 boneless chicken breats
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsps lemon pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
Directions:
  1. Place chciken breasts in a plastic bag with lemon juice, olive oil, lemon pepper, oregano, and salt and pepper. Let marinate for at least 30 minutes
  2. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees. Remove chicken from marinade and place on a baking sheet
  3. Bake chicken for 18-20 minutes until the internal temperature reaches 165. Serve and enjoy!

Ahi Tuna + Mango Salsa

This flavorful dish features pepper-seared ahi tuna complemented by a zesty mango salsa, offering a combination of bold flavors and nutrient-rich ingredients.

Ahi Tuna + Mango Salsa
Ingredients:
  • 2 tuna steaks
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 fresh mango, chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, chopped
  • 2 tbsp course black pepper
Directions:
  1. After marinating the tuna steaks in lime juice and seasoning, pack on course black pepper and sear 3 minutes on each side
  2. Combine the mango, jalapeno, lime juice, and cilantro to make the salsa
  3. Slice the tuna steaks and serve on top of the mango salsa, enjoy!

Ingredient Spotlight

BLUEBERRIES

Blueberries, renowned as a nutritional powerhouse, offer a multitude of benefits for overall wellness. Packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and fiber, these tiny fruits promote heart health, support cognitive function, and aid in maintaining healthy skin. The high levels of anthocyanins in blueberries, responsible for their vibrant color, contribute to their anti-inflammatory properties, potentially reducing the risk of chronic diseases.
